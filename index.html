Apostolic Core Team Ministry International

Apostolic Core Team Ministry International is a spirit-filled ministry focused on prayer, worship, the word, and divine encounters through the power of God.

Under the visionary leadership of Prophet Samuel Eghan Gorman, the ministry has grown into a vibrant community of believers committed to experiencing God's transformative power. Every service is an opportunity for supernatural encounters, prophetic declarations, and Spirit-led worship.

We believe in the apostolic mandate — to raise a generation that walks in the fullness of God's purpose, power, and promise.
